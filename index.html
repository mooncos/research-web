Marcos Gómez-Bracamonte

MSc EECS Student @ KTH. Recently: Radar Digital Processing Research Intern @ SSR-UPM

My name is Marcos Gómez Bracamonte (aka Marcos Gómez-Bracamonte) and I am a MSc Systems, Control and Robotics student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. Recently, I have developed work as a research intern at the Radar and Microwave Group of the Dept. of Signals, Systems and Radiocommunications (SSR) at the Polytechnic University of Madrid, advised by Prof. Jesús Grajal.

My research interests include Machine Learning for digital signal processing, Reinforcement Learning, radar signal processing, mmWave embedded radar sensing, wireless radar networks, real-time sensor networks, and embedded hardware. I have been recently involved in the field of ML-enhanced Radar Digital Signal Processing and Communication, working on detecting and analysing Parkinson's Disease through the patient's gait with real-time radar sensing networks.

I received my bachelor's degree in Telecommunications Engineering (spec. Electrical Engineering and Computer Science) with Honours, at the Polytechnic University of Madrid in 2022. I am currently pursuing a double master's degree in Telecommunications Engineering and EECS from Universidad Politécnica de Madrid and KTH Royal Institute of Technology.
