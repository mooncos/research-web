Marcos Gómez-Bracamonte

Radar Digital Processing Research Intern @ SSR-UPM

My name is Marcos Gómez Bracamonte (aka Marcos Gómez-Bracamonte) and I am a research intern at the Radar and Microwave Group of the Dept. of Signals, Systems and Radiocommunications (SSR) at the Polytechnic University of Madrid, advised by Prof. Jesús Grajal.

My research interests include radar signal processing, digital signal processing, mmWave embedded radar sensing, wireless radar networks, real-time sensor networks, and embedded radar hardware. I am currently focused on the field of Radar Digital Signal Processing and Communication, working on detecting and analysing Parkinson's Disease through the patient's gait with real-time radar sensing networks.

I received my bachelor's degree in Telecommunications Engineering (spec. Electrical Engineering and Computer Science) with Honours, at the Polytechnic University of Madrid in 2022. I am currently pursuing my master's degree in Telecommunications Engineering.
